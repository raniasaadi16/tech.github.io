Business Analyst training for Beginners

Entry Certificate in Business Analysis (ECBA) is an IIBA certification to help you become a business analyst. Techcanvass, being an Endorsed Education Provider (EEP) offers this ECBA certification course to help you meet the eligibility criteria. Designed as a completely practical and hands-on course, it will help you master industry-accepted practical business analysis skills like UML Modelling, SQL, User Stories, SRS preparation etc. The hands-on and interactive training will help you crack the business analyst interviews as well.

Why become a Business Analyst:

Business Analyst profile is one of the most in-demand profiles in the IT industry.

It’s open to even Non-IT professionals making it the only entry point in the IT industry.

Once you get through first 6-12 months, remuneration for BAs is quite rewarding as well as the growth.

    Course Projects

    Frinsley Bank – User stories and cards

    Frinsley Bank has recently got the banking license. The management wants to build an Internet banking system for retail customers. The bank has already implemented the core banking, credit cards and lending system implemented. The internet banking system is going to interface with the product systems (core banking, credit cards and lending system)

    Project Objectives: As a Business analyst you are responsible for the development of user stories and user story cards so that the features can be implemented.

    Car Zone – Context Diagram and Use Case Model

    Centip Trading International is a leading car selling company. It is now planning to enter used car sales and service. The company is planning to launch a platform to enable selling and buying of used cars. It will work as an intermediary for the buyers and sellers and provide its services for car registration, pricing, and other such services.

    Project Objectives: You have started the requirements elicitation meeting and would like to create early stage models for the project. Create the context diagram and use case model for the sellers and buyers modules. You can make assumptions about the features.
